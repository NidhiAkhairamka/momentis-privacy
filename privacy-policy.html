Your privacy matters. Momentis stores all your personal data locally on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

1. Information We Collect

Momentis collects the following information to provide you with a personalized fitness experience:

  • Account Information: Email address and name (for login and personalization)
  • Health & Fitness Data: Weight, height, age, body fat percentage, muscle mass, BMI
  • Workout Data: Exercise programs, workout logs, training history, RPE ratings
  • Preferences: Fitness goals, coach persona selection, notification settings

2. How We Store Your Data

All your personal data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. Your data never leaves your device except when using the AI Coach feature.

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data at rest on your device.

3. AI Coach Feature

When you use the AI Coach feature, your chat messages are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. This is necessary to provide intelligent, personalized fitness coaching.

  • Chat messages are processed ephemerally (not permanently stored by Google)
  • Data is encrypted in transit
  • No personal identifiers are included in API requests

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • AI Coach: Chat messages sent to Google Gemini API for coaching responses
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Edit: Update your profile information at any time
  • Delete: Clear all workout data via Settings ‚Üí Clear Workout Data
  • Export: Export your data using the Database Inspector feature
  • Account Deletion: Request complete account and data deletion

Momentis is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
